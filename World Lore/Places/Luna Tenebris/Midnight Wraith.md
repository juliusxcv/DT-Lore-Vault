## **Inquisitorial Report – Space Hulk Designate: “Midnight Wraith”**

**Document Code:** IX-XN/SH-998.88-VX 
**Classification:** Omega-Level Anomaly – Quarantined Asset 
**Filed By:** Magos-Explorator Thalor Vex, Ordo Xenos 
**Verified By:** [[Lord Inquisitor Lucius Harridan]] 
**Status:** Active – Containment Incomplete

### I. **General Overview**

The _Midnight Wraith_ is a macro-agglomeration of voidcraft wreckage, derelict vessels, and lost Mechanicus constructs fused together over millennia. It exhibits standard space hulk characteristics: erratic warp emergence, internal gravity zones, and partial atmosphere retention. The hulk’s mass exceeds 14.2 cubic kilometers, with over 300 identifiable hull fragments spanning multiple technological epochs.

The hulk materialized within the Bellum Eternum system on 998.M41.219, breaching standard warp detection protocols and bypassing orbital defense grids. It is currently embedded deep below the crater basin near [[Research Facility Tenebris]], within the core  of _[[Luna Tenebris]]_.

### II. **Discovery and Breach Event**

- **Discoverer:** [[Rogue Trader Lord Ephraim Veritax]]
- **Date of Boarding:** 998.M41.220
- **Objective:** Acquisition of pre-Imperial salvage and xenotech artifacts
- **Incident Summary:**
    - Veritax led a private boarding party into the central mass of the hulk.
    - Within the deepest segment, a structure of non-Imperial origin was located—designated **[[Citadel Obscura]]**.
    - Upon entering the citadel, Veritax breached a containment seal composed of salt and ash, arranged in a circular barrier.
    - This act released a dormant entity referred to in recovered texts as the **Dark Soul**, or a **Shard thereof**.
    - The entity initiated **Rift-Travel**, a non-warp transdimensional propulsion method, in an attempt to return to its origin universe.

### III. **Intervention and Materialization**

- **Mechanicus Response:**
    - An Adeptus Mechanicus Explorator fleet, already conducting a survey of the hulk’s outer layers, detected anomalous energy surges.
    - Emergency protocols were enacted to disable all warp-capable engines within the hulk.
    - The rift event was partially disrupted, resulting in the _Midnight Wraith_ collapsing into realspace and embedding itself within _Luna Tenebris_.
    - The hulk remains partially active, with internal systems fluctuating and psychic interference increasing.

### IV. **Current Status and Threat Assessment**

|**Threat Vector**|**Status**|**Notes**|
|---|---|---|
|Rift-Space Instability|Active|Local gravity distortions and time dilation observed|
|Dark Soul Influence|Confirmed|Corruption of Mechanicus personnel and fauna documented|
|Apostolic Manifestation|Unconfirmed|Glyphs and auditory hallucinations reported|
|Structural Integrity|Stable|Citadel Obscura remains sealed but reactive|
|Warp Engine Activity|Dormant|All known engines disabled; rift core remains volatile|

### V. **Containment Protocols**

- **Citadel Obscura** sealed under Protocol Umbra-Null
- **Midnight Wraith** tethered via orbital anchors and subterranean stabilizers
- **No extraction attempts authorized**
- **All personnel entering the hulk must undergo post-mission psychic screening and memory purge**
- **Inquisitor Harridan’s research logs remain classified and inaccessible**

### VI. **Strategic Recommendations**

- **Destruction of the hulk is not viable.** Nova Cannon simulations indicate insufficient yield.
- **Continued surveillance required.** Psychic resonance increasing at unpredictable intervals.
