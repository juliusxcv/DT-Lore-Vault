## **Inquisitorial Report – Artifact Designate: “Shard of the Dark Soul”**

**Document Code:** IX-XN/DS-998.77-HK **Classification:** Omega-Level Containment – Eyes Only **Filed By:** Inquisitor Harridan, Ordo Xenos **Status:** Active – Quarantined

### I. **Summary of Incident**

On 898.M41.223, the space hulk _[[Midnight Wraith]]_ materialized within the core of _Luna Tenebris_, breaching standard warp emergence protocols and bypassing all known detection grids. The hulk embedded itself within the moon’s crust, anchoring a massive citadel-like structure of unknown origin—designated **Citadel Obscura**— deep below the crater basin near Research Station Tenebris.

Initial scans revealed anomalous energy signatures, non-Euclidean architecture, and psychic interference consistent with **xeno-archaeotech corruption**. Within the citadel’s core, a sealed chamber was located containing a crystalline mass—later identified as a **Shard of the Dark Soul**, a transdimensional entity of unknown origin.

### II. **Artifact Profile: Shard of the Dark Soul**

- **Origin:** Extra-universal; not native to known warp-space or material reality
- **Composition:** Non-reactive crystal matrix; emits low-frequency psychic resonance
- **Behavior:** Passive containment; active corruption of surrounding matter and thought
- **Capabilities:**
    - Genetic manipulation of dead tissue
    - Psychic influence over susceptible minds
    - Rift-space distortion (non-warp FTL anomalies)
    - Long-range telepathic projection
    - Indirect control over mechanical systems (Dark Mechanicus infiltration suspected)

### III. **Historical Context (Redacted)**

Recovered fragments from pre-Imperial archives and forbidden texts suggest the **Dark Soul** is a primordial force fractured by the emergence of Light. The entity exists in cyclical phases of dormancy and devastation, deploying **Apostles**—semi-autonomous extensions of its will—to corrupt and consume sentient life.

The current shard is believed to be a remnant of the Dark Soul’s last defeat, sealed by an entity referred to as the **Ancient Enemy**, during a conflict predating the Great Crusade. The seal remains partially intact, though the shard continues to exert influence.

### IV. **Inquisitorial Involvement**

Inquisitor Harridan authorized covert experimentation on the shard, seeking to:

- Reanimate dead tissue for battlefield deployment
- Unlock **Rift-Travel**, a theoretical alternative to warp-based FTL
- Harness the shard’s mutagenic properties for controlled evolution

All experiments were terminated following mass psychogenic collapse of research personnel and the spontaneous activation of dormant systems within _Midnight Wraith_. Harridan has since been declared **missing in action**.

- **Citadel Obscura** sealed under Protocol Umbra-Null
- All access restricted to Omega-clearance operatives
- Vox silence enforced across Luna Tenebris
- No attempts to extract or destroy the shard authorized
