### I. **Overview**

Bellum Eternum Tertius is the third planet in the Bellum Eternum system, located within the Helios Sub-Cluster of Segmentum Solar. The planet is classified as a **Category II Warzone World**, having served as the primary theater of operations during the **Ork Pacification Campaigns** of M39 through early M41. While currently under Imperial quarantine, historical records suggest the planet may have played a **strategic role during the Great Crusade**, with multiple undocumented deployments and the possible presence of **unrecovered Warmachines** dating to the early expansion era.

### II. **Historical Utilization**

#### A. **Initial Survey and Strategic Designation (M30–M31)**

- Fragmentary records indicate Bellum Eternum Tertius was utilized as a forward staging ground during the Great Crusade.
- Deployment logs reference multiple Titan-class landings and Mechanicus excavation rites, though no surviving data confirms the full extent of operations.
- Planetary terrain includes vast trench networks, fortified ridgelines, and sub-surface vaults of unknown origin.
- Strategic designation was elevated to **High-Value Tactical Node**, later downgraded following the Horus Heresy.

#### B. **Ork Infestation and Imperial Response (M39.015–M40.221)**

- Ork presence first confirmed in M39.015 following the collapse of local agri-settlements.
- Full infestation reached planetary saturation by M39.022, prompting deployment of multiple Astra Militarum regiments under the **Bellum Eternum Pacification Mandate**.
- Combat operations spanned nearly two centuries, with significant losses on both sides.
- Notable engagements include:
    - The Siege of Hive Varnax (M39.088)
    - The Collapse of the Eastern Wastes (M39.201)
    - The Final Purge of the Iron Plateau (M40.219)
    
- Ork forces were declared pacified in M40.221, though residual activity remains unconfirmed in deep subterranean zones.

#### C. **Post-War Status and Quarantine (M40.222–Present)**

- Following cessation of hostilities, Bellum Eternum Tertius was placed under **Imperial Quarantine Protocol 7-B**, citing:
    - Unstable terrain and residual xenos contamination
    - Unexplained energy readings from sealed vaults
    - Psychological degradation among post-war survey teams
    
- Local planetary governance, headquartered at Bellum Eternum Prime, has declined all requests for further excavation or resource allocation.
- Adeptus Mechanicus petitions to investigate suspected Warmachine signatures have been denied or indefinitely deferred.
- All active military assets withdrawn by M40.229.
- Planet remains under passive orbital surveillance; no sanctioned surface operations permitted.
