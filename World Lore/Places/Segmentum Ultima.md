The **Segmentum Ultima**, also known as the **Ultima Segmentum**, is the [segmentum](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Segmentum "Segmentum") of [Imperial](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial "Imperial") space to the galactic east of [Terra](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Terra "Terra"). The Ultima Segmentum contains the uninhabitable core of the [Milky Way Galaxy](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Milky_Way_Galaxy "Milky Way Galaxy") which is marked by a super-massive black hole.

The Ultima Segmentum is home to the largest number of worlds in the [Imperium of Man](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Imperium_of_Man "Imperium of Man") as it is the largest of the segmentae in terms of its sheer size and spatial volume. It contains the [Ultramarines](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Ultramarines "Ultramarines")' [Realm of Ultramar](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Realm_of_Ultramar "Realm of Ultramar"). It has seen attacks by two separate [Tyranid](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Tyranid "Tyranid") [hive fleets](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Hive_fleet "Hive fleet"), including [Hive Fleet Behemoth](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Hive_Fleet_Behemoth "Hive Fleet Behemoth") and [Hive Fleet Kraken](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Hive_Fleet_Kraken "Hive Fleet Kraken").

The [Eastern Fringe](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Eastern_Fringe "Eastern Fringe") of the galaxy is adjacent to, but not officially a part of the Segmentum Ultima.

This region of the galaxy lies the most distant from the beacon of the [Astronomican](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Astronomican "Astronomican"). [Ork empires](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Ork_empire "Ork empire"), pirate conclaves, the expansionist [T'au Empire](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/T%27au_Empire "T'au Empire") and [Tyranid](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Tyranid "Tyranid") incursions are the main threat within this oft-volatile region of Imperial space.

The [Segmentum Fortress](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Segmentum_Fortress "Segmentum Fortress") of Segmentum Ultima is located on the world of [Kar Duniash](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Kar_Duniash "Kar Duniash"). It is the site of an [Imperial Navy](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Navy "Imperial Navy") academy and a major orbital shipyard that is the primary builder of the [_Dominator_-class Cruiser](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Dominator-class_Cruiser "Dominator-class Cruiser").

Since the creation of the [Great Rift](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Great_Rift "Great Rift") in the [Era Indomitus](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Era_Indomitus "Era Indomitus"), approximately half of the Segmentum Ultima now lies on the far side of the rift from [Terra](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Terra "Terra"), and is part of the _[Imperium Nihilus](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Imperium_Nihilus "Imperium Nihilus")_ where the light of the Astronomican is no longer easily visible to [Navigators](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Navigators "Navigators") and other [psykers](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Psykers "Psykers").


### Notable Regions
- **[Grendl Stars](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Grendl_Stars "Grendl Stars")** - The Grendl Stars are a constellation of stars located on the far [Eastern Fringe](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Eastern_Fringe "Eastern Fringe") of the Milky Way Galaxy that were once known as the Wings of Sanguinius. They are now known to harbour the hyper-violent xenos race called the [Barghesi](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Barghesi "Barghesi").

- **[Ghoul Stars](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Ghoul_Stars "Ghoul Stars")** - This region of space is located beyond the furthest reaches of known space, northeast of the [Eastern Fringe](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Eastern_Fringe "Eastern Fringe"). This vast tract of relatively unexplored wilderness space was once home to a number of inhabited human worlds. However, an ancient threat, known only as the [Pale Wasting](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Pale_Wasting "Pale Wasting") in Imperial records, is believed to have been responsible for the destruction of these worlds' populations during the [Nova Terra Interregnum](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Nova_Terra_Interregnum "Nova Terra Interregnum"). This accounts for the large number of [Dead Worlds](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Dead_Worlds "Dead Worlds") within the region. The Ghoul Stars region lies beyond the light of the [Astronomican](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Astronomican "Astronomican"), which is used by Imperial [Navigators](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Navigators "Navigators") to guide vessels while travelling in the [Warp](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Warp "Warp"). Without the guidance of this vital beacon, Navigators are unable to sense its guiding light and are forced to navigate blindly. Navigators often refuse to travel into this region for this very reason, for it is extremely hazardous to do so. The [Death Spectres](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Spectres "Death Spectres") [Space Marine](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Space_Marine "Space Marine") [Chapter](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Chapter "Chapter") currently maintains a constant vigil to ensure that the inhabitants of the Ghoul Stars never again threaten the galaxy.

- **[Charadon](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Charadon "Charadon") ([Ork Empire](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Ork_Empire "Ork Empire"))** - Charadon is the capital world of the largest and longest-standing [Ork](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Ork "Ork") empire in the galaxy, also known as Charadon, which is ruled by the tyrannical Ork [Warlord](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Warlord "Warlord") [Snagrod](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Snagrod "Snagrod") the Arch-Arsonist, a particularly vicious and sadistic leader of a race noted for its generally vicious and sadistic tendencies.

- **Octarius ([Ork Empire](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Ork_Empire "Ork Empire"))** - Octarius is an Ork empire covering the Octarius Sector, an area of space almost as large as the Realm of [Ultramar](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Ultramar "Ultramar"), centred on the Ork World of Octarius. It is ruled by a vicious Ork Warlord known as the Overfiend of Octarius.

- **[Realm of Ultramar](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Realm_of_Ultramar "Realm of Ultramar")** - The feudal demesne comprising 11 worlds which is administered by the [Ultramarines](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Ultramarines "Ultramarines") [Chapter](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Chapter "Chapter") of [Space Marines](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Space_Marines "Space Marines").

- **Sautekh Dynasty** - The Sautekh Dynasty is a [Necron](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Necron "Necron") dynasty consisting of 80 [Tomb Worlds](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Tomb_World "Tomb World"). Its Crown World is Mandragora. This powerful Necron dynasty is currently ruled over by [Phaeron](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Phaeron "Phaeron") [Imotekh](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Imotekh "Imotekh"), "the Stormlord."

- **[T'au Empire](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/T%27au_Empire "T'au Empire")** - The T'au Empire is a rapidly expanding xenos stellar empire situated within the Imperium of Man's Segmentum Ultima, near the [Eastern Fringe](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Eastern_Fringe "Eastern Fringe") of the [Milky Way Galaxy](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Milky_Way_Galaxy "Milky Way Galaxy").

- **[Triplex Sector](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Triplex_Sector "Triplex Sector")** - The Triplex Sector is dominated by swathes of barren star systems, largely uninhabitable and empty. It is a wasteland of ore-rich asteroids and older, dying stars where a few scattered clans of [Abhuman](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Abhuman "Abhuman") miners and prospectors try to eke out a living. However, it still contains some places of interest to the [Imperium](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Imperium "Imperium") in the [Eastern Fringe](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Eastern_Fringe "Eastern Fringe").

- **[Korianis Sector](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Korianis_Sector "Korianis Sector")** - Contains the [Sub-sector Aurelia](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Sub-sector_Aurelia "Sub-sector Aurelia"), home of the [Blood Ravens](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_Ravens "Blood Ravens") [Chapter](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Chapter "Chapter") of the [Adeptus Astartes](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Adeptus_Astartes "Adeptus Astartes"), and site of the three [Aurelian Crusades](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Aurelian_Crusades "Aurelian Crusades").

- **[Elara's Veil](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Elara%27s_Veil "Elara's Veil")** - Elara's Veil is a massive, crimson-coloured nebula located somewhere in the [Ultima Segmentum](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Ultima_Segmentum "Ultima Segmentum") of the [galaxy](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Galaxy "Galaxy") that is also an isolated [sector](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Sector "Sector") of the [Imperium](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Imperium "Imperium"). It is named after the legendary hero Elara, a significant figure in the mythology of the people of the [Ocean World](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Ocean_World "Ocean World") of [Nemeton](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Nemeton "Nemeton"). Comprised of twenty [Human](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Human "Human")-settled [worlds](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Worlds "Worlds"), Elara's Veil falls under the protection of the three [Chapters](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Chapters "Chapters") of the [Adeptus Astartes](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Adeptus_Astartes "Adeptus Astartes") known as the [Adeptus Vaelarii](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Adeptus_Vaelarii "Adeptus Vaelarii"), the "Sentinels of the Veil," which once included the [Emperor's Spears](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Emperor%27s_Spears "Emperor's Spears"), the [Celestial Lions](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Celestial_Lions "Celestial Lions") and the [Star Scorpions](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Scorpions "Star Scorpions") before the latter was destroyed and corrupted by [Chaos](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos").
