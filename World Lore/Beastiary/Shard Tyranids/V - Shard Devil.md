###  Lore:

> _"It was supposed to be a beast of muscle and instinct. Now it whispers in broken tongues, its carapace glistening with unnatural geometry. The jungle doesn’t fear it—it worships it."_

Once a native terror of Catachan, this Devil was exposed to a fragment of the Shard during one of the many experiments with the [[Shard]]. Severed from nature and fused with alien intelligence, it now acts as a living conduit for the Shard’s will—twisted, unstable, and utterly lethal.

###  Boss Traits:

- **Size**: Monstrous (fills half the board)
- **Behavior**: Semi-intelligent, erratic, and reactive
- **Environment**: Jungle terrain with shifting crystal growths and psychic storms

### Abilities:

  - **Crystal Carapace** 
    - Reduces all incoming damage by 1 (min 1)
    - On a 6+ save, reflects 1 mortal wound to attacker
        
- **Shard Roar (Psychic)** 
    - Once per round, all models within 12" must pass Leadership test
    - Fail = stunned (no actions except movement)
        
- **Tail of Ruin** 
    - Sweeping attack in 3" arc
    - D3 damage per model, ignores cover
        
- **Burrow Ambush** 
    - Once per Phase can disappear underground and reappear anywhere next turn
    - Causes terrain instability (roll D6 for nearby terrain: on 1–2, collapse)
        
- **Mutation Surge** 
    - Triggers at 15 HP
    - Gains +2 ATK, MV becomes 12"
    - All terrain within 6" becomes hazardous (D3 mortal wounds on entry)

## Boss Fight Mechanics

### Phase Shifts:
- **Phase 1 (30–16 HP)**
    - Predictable movement
    - Focused melee aggression
    - Players can kite or bait

- **Phase 2 (15–6 HP)**
    - Mutation Surge activates
    - Terrain becomes deadly
    - Psychic Roar intensifies
    - Movement becomes erratic

- **Phase 3 (5–0 HP)**
    - Frenzy Mode: attacks twice per turn
    - Ignores terrain
    - Immune to stun and suppression
    - Gains +1 DMG on all melee attacks

####  **Environmental Hazards**

-  **Shard Spikes**
    - At end of each round, D3 crystal growths erupt randomly
    - Block movement, deal 1 mortal wound on contact
        
- **Psychic Storms**
    - Every 3 turns, roll D6 for each model
    - On 1–2, take D3 mortal wounds from psychic feedback


## Victory Condition: **Shard Seal Protocol**

> _“The Devil is not just a beast. It’s a beacon. To kill it, you must silence the signal.”_

### Puzzle Mechanic:

- **Three Shard Nodes** are scattered across the battlefield (place in strategic terrain zones)
- Each node must be **destroyed** before the Devil can take **full damage**
- While nodes are active:
    - Devil takes **half damage** from all sources
    - Gains **+1 DEF**
    - Can use **Burrow Ambush** every turn

### Node Destruction:

- Each node has:
    - **HP: 6**, **DEF: 4+**
    - Immune to psychic attacks
    - Can only be damaged by **melee or explosives**
        
- When a node is destroyed:
    - Devil loses one ability (chosen randomly)
    - Psychic Storm intensity increases

### Final Blow:

- Once all three nodes are destroyed:
    - Devil becomes vulnerable
    - Crystal Carapace deactivates
    - Players can deal full damage and trigger Phase 3
