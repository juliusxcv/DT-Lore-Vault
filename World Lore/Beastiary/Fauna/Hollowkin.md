## **Folktale Fragment – “The Moving Shadow”**

**Source:** Settler Logbook – Crater Varnax, M39.014 **Recovered By:** Archivist-Magos Tertullian Vex **Status:** Cultural Artifact – Non-Canonical

> _“They say the light here bends wrong. That it doesn’t fall—it crawls._ _And when the dusk comes, and the trees begin to hum, you mustn’t look too long._ _Because if you stare into the shadows, they stare back._ _And if you blink, they move.”_

> _“Old Maren saw one once. Said it was tall, thin, like a man made of smoke and glass._ _Said it shimmered like oil, and vanished when she turned her head._ _Said it sang to her—not with words, but with her bones.”_

> _“We called them the Hollowkin. Because they never speak. And they never stay.”_


## **Xenoform Profile – Hollowkin**

**Imperial Designation:** XN-TNBR/PRIM-001 **Common Name:** _Hollowkin_ **Classification:** Native Xenoform – Non-Aggressive **Intelligence Tier:** Tier II – Primitive Cognitive Function **Threat Level:** Minimal (Passive Avoidance Behavior)

### I. **Physical Description**

- **Stature:** 2.2–2.6 meters tall
- **Build:** Slender, long-limbed, digitigrade locomotion
- **Skin:** Black, shimmering surface with refractive properties; appears oily or glass-like under Luna Tenebris light
- **Facial Features:** None visible; cranial structure smooth and featureless
- **Camouflage:**
    - Perfect passive camouflage without technological aid
    - Visible only in peripheral vision; direct observation renders them imperceptible
    - Movement triggers minor light distortion detectable by trained observers

### II. **Behavioral Traits**

- **Disposition:** Shy, reclusive, non-confrontational
- **Habitat:** Dense forest zones, subterranean biospheres, crater ridgelines
- **Social Structure:** Solitary or small familial clusters
- **Communication:**
    - Emits low-frequency vibrations (subsonic range)
    - Believed to communicate through environmental resonance—trees, soil, and wind patterns
    - Some settlers report “feeling” their presence before seeing them

### III. **Ecological Role**

- **Biosphere Integration:**
    - Symbiotic relationship with native flora
    - Believed to regulate fungal growth and soil nutrient cycles
    - Avoids artificial structures and electromagnetic fields

- **Cultural Impact:**
    - Early settlers recorded multiple sightings, often during eclipse cycles
    - Rituals developed to “appease the shadows,” including silent vigils and salt circles
    - No confirmed hostile interactions; Hollowkin retreat when approached

### IV. **Inquisitorial Notes**

> _“The Hollowkin are not a threat. They are a question._ _They do not speak. They do not fight. They do not flee._ _They simply watch. And wait._ _For what, we do not know.”_

> _“Their camouflage is not deception. It is protection._ _From us. From the hulk. From the shard.”_!
