
## LVL 1 Autopsy:
The designated Mindspike Horror consists solely of a severed human cranial unit, invariably originating from psyker subjects. The neck stump displays no evidence of natural cauterization or healing, yet remains biologically active through extensive necrotic reinforcement. Spider-like locomotor limbs emerge from fused spinal and cranial tissue, allowing the specimen independent movement despite the absence of a conventional body.

Most disturbing is the persistent vocalization.

Even while sedated, dissected, or rendered immobile, the specimen emits continuous screaming—fluctuating between psychic discharge, involuntary pain response, and fragmented vocal patterns.

## LVL 2 Autopsy:
Internal examination confirms deliberate manufacture.

The procedure appears to require:
- A living or recently deceased psyker head
- Mutagenic fluid extracted from Undead Cadaver tissue
- A sharpened rod made of the same glimmering material found in the Aberrant and Rift Leaper, implanted directly through the cranial cavity into the brain stem

The mutagenic compound arrests death while inducing radical tissue adaptation, preserving psychic functionality beyond biological viability. The embedded Shard rod serves not as support but as an active resonance conductor to the necrotic signal emanating from beneath Luna Tenebris

## LVL 3 Autopsy:
Residual mind-state analysis suggests catastrophic cognitive collapse.

Unlike Legionnaires or Mutants, traces of the original psyche remain intensely active. Psychic echoes recovered through dampened auspex indicate recurring experiences of:

Falling, Burning, Endless pursuit. Repetitive pleas for death or silence

The Shard rod appears to prevent final dissolution while simultaneously amplifying warp sensitivity. Subjects are effectively trapped between the predatory pressures of the Warp and the invasive resonance of the Moon itself, unable to die yet incapable of coherent existence.

This explains the constant screaming.

The Warp pulls at them.
The Moon pulls harder.

## LVL 4 Autopsy:
Recovered laboratory fragments attribute the Mindspike project directly to Harridan and the Magos Biologis’ later experimentation into psychic warfare. Initial goals focused on creating stable anti-psyker organisms capable of disrupting warp activity and shielding Tenebris from hostile psychic intrusion.

Early trials failed catastrophically.

Rather than producing controlled nullification, the Shard rods transformed subjects into living resonance amplifiers. Warp disturbances intensified around them, psychic abilities became erratic, and surrounding personnel reported hallucinations, intrusive thoughts, and spontaneous neural hemorrhaging.
