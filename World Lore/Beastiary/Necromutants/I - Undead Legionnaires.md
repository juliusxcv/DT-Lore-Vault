### Abilities:
-  **Braindead**: Undead Legionnaires possess no capacity to think for themselves and need at least 1 Tier II Necromutant on the Table. If no Tier II Necromutant is present they lose  all abilities.
- **Mindless Advance**:  Cannot perform complex actions (e.g., objectives, overwatch). Always moves toward nearest enemy in LOS or audio source unless controlled by a [[Synaptic Node Core]].
- **Infect**:  On a critical hit, target suffers -1 DEF until end of next round.
- **Swarm Hunger**:  If 3 or more Undead Legionnaire are within 2" of the same target, all gain +1 ATK and +1 DMG.
- **Corpse Magnet**:  At the end of each round, roll a D6 for each dead model within 3". On a 6, spawn a new Undead Legionnaire at half HP.
