Drukhari / [[Shards of Venom]]

**Role:** Ally from the Cult of the Razorpetal, brought in to ensure dominance in melee combat and provide terrifying displays for the arenas.
   
**Personality:** Frenzied and theatrical in battle, reveling in the visceral spectacle of her kills.
   
**Motivation:** Yrellith is promised first choice of the captured gene-spliced beasts for her gladiatorial games, as well as opportunities to perform grand executions using the creatures in the arenas.
