Drukhari / [[Shards of Venom]]

**Role:** Leader of the beast tamers, responsible for capturing and subjugating the wildlife.
   
**Personality:** Sadistic and possessive over his beasts, viewing them as living weapons to be broken and controlled.
   
**Motivation:** Kaeryth’s driving goal is to add the Imperium’s gene-spliced creatures to his personal menagerie, cementing his reputation as the finest beastmaster in Commorragh.
