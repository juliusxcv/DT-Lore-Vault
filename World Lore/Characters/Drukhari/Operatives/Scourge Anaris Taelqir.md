Drukhari / [[Shards of Venom]]

**Role:** Aerial reconnaissance and sniper specialist, providing cover and mobility.
  
**Personality:** Aloof and disdainful, viewing ground-bound Drukhari as lesser beings.
   
**Motivation:** Anaris serves mainly for profit and the promise of hunting rights over the most dangerous prey during the raid. She also dreams of using the gene-spliced beasts as trophies to win favor in the pinnacle roosts of the Scourges.
