Drukhari / [[Shards of Venom]]

**Role:** Master fleshcrafter and overseer of any biological assets the party captures.
  
**Personality:** Detached, macabre, and fascinated by the fusion of xenos genetics with tortured flesh.
   
**Motivation:** Draszhar is intrigued by the gene-splicer artifact's potential to enhance his grotesque experiments. He dreams of creating abominations so horrifying they would be revered as art in the dark halls of Commorragh.
