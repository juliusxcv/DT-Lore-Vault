**Shadow Sentinel Imperial Guard Regiment**


### Background
The Shadow Sentinels are an elite regiment of the Astra Militarum, forged in secrecy and shrouded in mystery. Stationed in the distant reaches of the Segmentum Ultima, this regiment is tasked with a singular and perilous mission: to defend the isolated and clandestine research outpost of **[[Luna Tenebris]]**.

[[Research Facility Tenebris]], nestled within the shadowy depths of an ancient impact crater, is a secretive research facility where the Adeptus Mechanicus conducts dangerous experiments with xenotechnology and warp anomalies. The station’s existence is known only to a select few within the Imperium, and its protection is of utmost priority, for within its walls lies technology that could turn the tide of war or unravel the fabric of reality itself.

The Shadow Sentinels were handpicked from the most resilient and cunning veterans across countless campaigns. Each member of the regiment has proven their mettle in the fires of war, earning commendations for their exceptional skills in guerrilla warfare, reconnaissance, and sabotage. Their armor, a light matte blue, is equipped with advanced refractor fields that render them nearly invisible in the shadows, allowing them to strike swiftly and vanish without a trace.

Their leader, Colonel Alera Vandis, is a tactical genius known for her unyielding resolve and strategic brilliance. Under her command, the Shadow Sentinels have repelled countless assaults from xenos raiders, and even the occasional inquisitive Aeldari seeking the secrets of Tenebris Station. Despite the myriad threats, the regiment remains steadfast, their loyalty to the Emperor and their duty unwavering.

Within the regiment, there are whispers of a hidden agenda. Some speculate that the Shadow Sentinels are not just defending the research outpost, but also ensuring that its secrets never fall into the wrong hands, even if it means purging the facility themselves. This underlying tension only adds to the regiment's enigmatic aura, making them both feared and respected by those who know of their existence.
