#### Abilities

**Twilight Ambush**
When attacking from cover or concealment, the Shadow Sentinels gain bonuses to hit and damage. +1 to DMG

**Adaptive Camouflage**
If the operative has not moved, the adaptive cloak can be activated. When activated saving throws are 3+

**Void-Hardened Discipline**
They are immune to fear effects and receive bonuses to resist psychic attacks and mental corruption.

**Coordinated Fire Protocol**
By focusing fire on a single target, the Shadow Sentinels can increase their collective accuracy and damage. When 3 or more Sentinels shoot the same target the consequent Sentinel get +1 ATK for Ranged Attacks.

### Interactions / Dialogue Options

**Greeting:** 
>"About damn time someone showed up. I was beginning to think the brass didn’t care if we lived or died."

**What is this Place?:**
>"This place? It’s wrong, plain and simple. Sure, the skies are pretty, but it’s all a distraction. The crater basin traps you, and the forest plays tricks on your eyes. Pines so tall you can’t see the sun—not that it ever rises properly. And those abandoned mining sites? Full of noise, like whispers carried on the wind." (_Grimaces, gripping his weapon_) "Once, during patrol, I swear I saw footprints heading out of an old shaft. Too big to be human. Whatever crawled out of the Maw still walks this moon. We haven’t seen it... yet."

**What happened here?:** 
>"We’ve been picking off stragglers from whatever broke loose down there. Weird things, xeno-looking, but not any species I’ve seen. They don’t fight like normal enemies—they move wrong."

**Have you seen the Killteam?** 
>"The Deathwatch? Yeah, I saw them. Big, black armor, swords and bolters blazing. They made it look easy, while we’re stuck here in the mud. Guess that’s what being a legend gets you."

**Closing Statement:** 
>"If you need a guide, I know every inch of this perimeter. Just don’t expect me to hold your hand."

**When asked about the Zombies/Undead Legionnaires:**
>Plagues spread sideways. This spreads _up_. Something down there is producing them.

**When asked about the Miners:**
>If you’re trying to understand why they left, the houses might help.
