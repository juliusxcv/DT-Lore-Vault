#### Abilities

**Twilight Ambush**
When attacking from cover or concealment, the Shadow Sentinels gain bonuses to hit and damage. +1 to DMG

**Adaptive Camouflage**
If the operative has not moved, the adaptive cloak can be activated. When activated saving throws are 3+

**Void-Hardened Discipline**
They are immune to fear effects and receive bonuses to resist psychic attacks and mental corruption.

**Coordinated Fire Protocol**
By focusing fire on a single target, the Shadow Sentinels can increase their collective accuracy and damage. When 3 or more Sentinels shoot the same target the consequent Sentinel get +1 ATK for Ranged Attacks.

>"Well, look at this lot. Thought I’d seen every kind of misfit on this Emperor-forsaken rock, but you’re a new breed."

**What is this Place?:**
>"Ah, the moon. Permanent twilight, soft hues like a painter’s fever dream. Makes you want to sit back with a cup of recaf, if it weren’t crawling with horrors. The forest? Temperate, sure, but don’t let those pines fool you—they’re hiding things. Once, I saw something move behind the trees during patrol: tall, skeletal, and silent. Didn’t stick around to make friends." (_Chuckles bitterly_) 
>
>"The old mining pits are worse. Found one full of bones—human ones, picked clean. And the caverns? Forget it. The lads call them the Maw. Whatever came up from there, let’s hope it doesn’t decide it wants a second helping."

>"We’ve been short on food, ammo, and sanity since the lockdown. Oh, and our vox tower? Useless. Guess they don’t teach tech-priesting in basic training."
