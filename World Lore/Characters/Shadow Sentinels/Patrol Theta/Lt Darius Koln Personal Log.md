### Personal Log – Lt. Darius Koln

**Theta Gate Company, Shadow Sentinels**  
**Locker ID: TH-Δ-117**

---

#### Log I – Initial Assessment

> Theta Gate remains operational.
> 
> Personnel morale is acceptable, though strained by prolonged isolation and poor light conditions. I have submitted a routine request for lamp recalibration and circadian adjustments.
> 
> Lower-level access remains restricted. Mechanicus assurances state that all experiments are within acceptable tolerance. I see no reason to doubt them.
> 
> Civilians in the mining habs have been fully evacuated. Some refused to leave personal belongings behind. This is understandable. Frontier families grow attached to places they should not.

---

#### Log II – Minor Irregularities

> Patrols have reported movement in sealed service corridors beneath Theta Gate. Auspex readings inconclusive. Likely vermin or structural settling.
> 
> I have instructed the men not to speculate. Speculation leads to fear, and fear erodes discipline.
> 
> I find it increasingly difficult to sleep. The light cycles are incorrect. Nights feel too long, days too short. I have begun keeping this log to organize my thoughts.

---

#### Log III – Civilian Records

> While reviewing abandoned habs near the outer dig site, I discovered several handwritten journals left behind by miner families.
> 
> They are… repetitive. Complaints about shadows, about children speaking in their sleep, about the sky feeling “too close.”
> 
> None of it is actionable. Still, I have secured a few volumes in my locker for later review. There is value in understanding civilian perception, even if it is flawed.

---

#### Log IV – Escalation

> Theta Gate vox experienced a brief echo during routine check-in. My own voice returned to me a fraction of a second late.
> 
> Tech-priests insist this is magnetic interference. I accepted their explanation. I always do.
> 
> One of my sentries asked if the lower levels were “awake.” I reprimanded him for phrasing. Gates are not alive.

---

#### Log V – First Breach

> Unauthorized access attempt registered from _below_ Theta Gate. Clearance codes matched decommissioned civilian tags.
> 
> That should not be possible.
> 
> I ordered a purge of the logs and instructed my men to double watches. I did not inform higher command. There was no proof. Only… coincidence.

---

#### Log VI – Personal Note

> I reread the journals tonight.
> 
> One child wrote that the light was “watching” her when she tried to sleep. Another family recorded hearing footsteps after curfew—always stopping outside their door.
> 
> I used to dismiss this kind of thing. I still do.
> 
> And yet, I have started locking my door.

---

#### Log VII – Lockdown Imminent

> Orders have come down. Full lockdown. No explanation given.
> 
> The Mechanicus assures me this is precautionary. They always use that word.
> 
> I can hear movement in the shafts now. Not loud. Not fast. Persistent.
> 
> I have instructed Theta Gate Company to hold position. We will not abandon our post.

---

#### Log VIII – Final Entry

> They are not breaching the gate.
> 
> They are waiting.
> 
> I understand now why the miners wrote so much. When no one listens, you leave words behind in the hope that someone will read them later and _believe you_.
