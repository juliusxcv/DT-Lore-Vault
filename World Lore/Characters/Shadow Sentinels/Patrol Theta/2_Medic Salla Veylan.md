#### Abilities

**Medic**
If this Unit administers Med Stims to an Operative, the Med Stims are more effective and heal +2 Wounds.

**Twilight Ambush**
When attacking from cover or concealment, the Shadow Sentinels gain bonuses to damage. +1 to DMG

**Adaptive Camouflage**
If the operative has not moved, the adaptive cloak can be activated. When activated saving throws are 3+

**Void-Hardened Discipline**
They are immune to fear effects and receive bonuses to resist psychic attacks and mental corruption.

**Coordinated Fire Protocol**
By focusing fire on a single target, the Shadow Sentinels can increase their collective accuracy and damage. When 3 or more Sentinels shoot the same target the consequent Sentinel get +1 ATK for Ranged Attacks.

### Interactions / Dialogue Options

**Greeting:** 
>"Please tell me you’ve brought med supplies. I’ve been stitching wounds with shrapnel and prayer for days."

**What is this Place?:**
>"This moon feels timeless. The sky shifts from blue to violet, like dusk stretching on forever. I sometimes catch myself staring at the horizon—it’s almost peaceful, isn’t it? But then I remember where we are. The crater basin is like a cage, and the forest presses in too close. Pines stretch so high, they block the stars." (_Looks down sadly_) "During patrols, we’ve found signs of the miners who came before us. Scattered equipment, rusting in the undergrowth. One patrol stumbled upon a miner’s datapad—talk of shadows in the caverns, things that hunted them. It’s no wonder they abandoned this place."

**What happened here?:** 
>"We’ve lost so many... and the screams coming from below—Emperor preserve us, it’s like the dead are clawing at the gates. We’ve heard nothing from the command staff since the artifact broke loose."

**Have you seen the Killteam?** 
>"The Deathwatch... they didn’t stop, not even for a second. It was like they knew exactly where to go. I thought they might help us evacuate the wounded, but no. They had their mission, and we weren’t part of it."

**Closing Statement:** 
>"If you’re going in there, promise me you’ll make it count. Too many have died for this moon already."
