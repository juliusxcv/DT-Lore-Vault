[[Lord Inquisitor Seraphina Valeria]] requested a Killteam to investigate [[Research Facility Tenebris]] for Traces of [[Lord Inquisitor Lucius Harridan]] and the Artifact he supposedly was chasing. Velatum Umbra was assigned for this Mission.

> Watch Commander Vorth,
> By Inquisitorial authority and under Standing Accord Xenos-2279-A ("Raxos Edict"), I submit this priority request for the deployment of a compact, highly specialized Deathwatch Killteam to investigate an incident of strategic and xeno-archaeological concern.
> 
> **Target Zone:** Luna Tenebris Research Facility, orbital fragment – Bellum Eternum System  **‍**
> **Last Contact:** Confirmed 10.018.869.M41 – standard Ordo Xenos reconnaissance killteam (Ref: K-Tauros)  **‍**
> **Nature of Loss:** All signals ceased during descent into Sublevel Epsilon. No breach alert registered. Auspex feed terminated without anomaly trace. Psychic residue inconsistent with Warp exposure but aligned with deep-techno interference.
> 
> I suspect pre-Imperial constructs or obfuscated xenos architectures beneath the station. Harshly shielded. Mechanicus logs are compromised and clearly redacted at high tiers, some bearing Harridan’s old cipher.
> I require a team capable of silent incursion, artifact containment, hostile engagement, and internal observation. I trust Deathwatch vigilance to preserve truth where standard Astartes protocol may incinerate it.
> 
> Respond with readiness confirmation or refusal under doctrinal justification.
> 
> For the Emperor’s silence and flame,  — _Seraphina Valeria_  _Lord Inquisitor, Ordo Xenos_


**Brother-Sergeant Elias Vorthan (Ultramarines, Team Leader)**

- **Personality:** Disciplined, strategic, and unwavering in his dedication to the Codex Astartes. Vorthan is a natural leader who prioritizes the mission above all else, but he often clashes with the more zealous or secretive members of the team.
    
- **Motivation:** Elias seeks to uncover the truth of the artifact and its role in the corruption overtaking the base. His leadership is the glue holding the team together, but he wrestles with the enormity of the situation and doubts their chances of survival.
    
- **Key Conflict:** As the team ventures deeper into the spacehulk, Elias begins to question whether the Codex alone can guide them against the horrors they face.


**Brother Arcenan Kael (Dark Angels, Interrogator-Chaplain)**

- **Personality:** Quiet, reserved, and inscrutable, Kael is the shadow lurking in the team’s midst. His words carry the weight of authority, but his motives are shrouded in secrecy. He often strays from the group under the pretense of reconnaissance.
    
- **Motivation:** Unknown to the rest of the team, Kael is not just here for the artifact. He has received intelligence that suggests this spacehulk contains remnants of **the Fallen**, traitors to the Dark Angels' chapter. His ulterior motive is to recover evidence of their presence—destroying or burying it as necessary—no matter the cost.
    
- **Key Conflict:** Kael’s secret agenda leads to his death when he breaks formation to investigate signs of the Drukhari. His obsession with the Fallen blinds him to the ambush closing in from the shadows.


**Brother Sorian Tael (Blood Ravens)**

- **Personality:** Inquisitive, intellectual, and deeply intuitive, Tael is fascinated by the artifact and the corrupted forces surrounding it. He constantly takes notes and records his observations, and his insights often prove critical to the team’s survival.
    
- **Motivation:** Sorian’s curiosity drives him to understand the artifact's purpose and origin. Unlike the others, he feels drawn to the alien cathedral, sensing there’s more to this mission than extermination. His cautionary advice, however, is often ignored by the others.
    
- **Key Conflict:** Sorian’s growing obsession with the artifact mirrors the dangers of meddling with the unknown. While he maintains his loyalty to the team, his curiosity borders on dangerous heresy in the eyes of more devout members like the Black Templar.
    

**Brother Viktor Kheldan (Black Templars)**

- **Personality:** Zealous, uncompromising, and utterly devoted to the Emperor. Kheldan’s faith is his weapon, and he sees the artifact as a vile abomination that must be destroyed at all costs. His contempt for xenos and their corruption is matched only by his suspicion of his team.
    
- **Motivation:** Kheldan believes their mission is divinely ordained—a test of their faith and endurance. He distrusts both Kael’s secrecy and Tael’s fascination with the artifact, but he is driven to see this mission through to its righteous conclusion.
    
- **Key Conflict:** As tensions rise within the team, Kheldan’s unwavering zeal threatens to fracture their unity. He constantly challenges Elias’s strategic decisions, believing that any hesitation is a sign of weakness.
    

**Brother Garrik Voln (Imperial Fists, Heavy Support Specialist)**

- **Personality:** Stoic, methodical, and pragmatic. Voln is the team’s anchor, providing firepower and reinforcement in moments of crisis. He rarely speaks, but when he does, his words carry weight.
    
- **Motivation:** Voln values duty above all else. While he lacks the philosophical leanings of the others, he believes in the importance of containment. His primary concern is ensuring nothing from the base escapes to threaten the wider Imperium.
    
- **Key Conflict:** Voln’s practicality often puts him at odds with Kheldan’s zeal and Kael’s secrecy. As the mission descends into chaos, he becomes increasingly aware of the futility of their efforts but continues to fight regardless.
