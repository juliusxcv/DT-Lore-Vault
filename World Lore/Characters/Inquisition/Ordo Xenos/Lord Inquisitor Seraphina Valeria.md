Born into a noble family on the hive world of Triach IV, Seraphina Valeria was destined for greatness from the moment of her birth. Her family, known for their unwavering loyalty to the Imperium and their contributions to the Astra Militarum, instilled in her a deep sense of duty and a fierce determination to protect humanity. From a young age, Seraphina exhibited a keen intellect, an insatiable curiosity, and an unyielding resolve—traits that would serve her well in the dark and dangerous future that awaited her.

As a child, Seraphina was captivated by tales of heroism and valor, particularly those involving the mysterious and enigmatic Inquisition. She devoured every scrap of knowledge she could find about the Ordo Xenos, the shadowy order dedicated to rooting out and exterminating the alien threat that lurked in the far corners of the galaxy. Her fascination soon turned into a calling, and at the age of fifteen, she petitioned to join the Inquisition.

Her request did not go unnoticed. [[Lord Inquisitor Lucius Harridan]], a seasoned member of the Ordo Xenos, saw potential in the young noble and took her under his wing. Under his tutelage, Seraphina underwent rigorous training, honing her skills in xenos lore, combat, and investigation. She traveled to distant worlds, witnessed the horrors of xenos incursions firsthand, and fought alongside some of the most elite warriors of the Imperium. Her experiences forged her into a formidable and resourceful inquisitor, ready to face the challenges that lay ahead.

After years of diligent service, Seraphina earned the title of Lord Inquisitor, a rare and prestigious honor bestowed upon only the most exceptional members of the [[Ordo Xenos]]. Now a respected and influential figure, she was assigned to the [[Segmentum Ultima]], a region plagued by xenos threats and political instability. Her mission: to eradicate the alien menace and protect the Imperium's borders from incursion.

With her flagship, the _Imperialis Aegis_, Seraphina and her retinue of loyal aides and acolytes embarked on countless missions to purge the segmentum of its xenos infestations. From the treacherous jungles of Rho'shar Prime to the desolate wastelands of Tarsis Ultra, she faced down the myriad alien threats with unwavering courage and tactical brilliance. Her reputation for ruthlessness and her unyielding determination earned her both respect and fear among her peers and enemies alike.

Yet, despite her success, Seraphina remains haunted by the shadow of her mentor, [[Lord Inquisitor Lucius Harridan]], who vanished under mysterious circumstances during a mission on the forsaken world of Kyrax II. She is driven not only by her duty to the Emperor but also by a personal quest to uncover the truth behind his disappearance and bring those responsible to justice.

In the grim darkness of the far future, Lord Inquisitor Seraphina Valeria stands as a beacon of hope and vigilance, ever watchful against the encroaching darkness. Her unwavering resolve and relentless pursuit of the alien threat ensure that the Imperium remains safe, even if it means sacrificing everything she holds dear.
