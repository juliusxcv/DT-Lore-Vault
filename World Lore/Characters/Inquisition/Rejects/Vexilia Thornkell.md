• Ideological Subversion: Vexilia’s personal creed—an amalgam of devout service and dangerously unorthodox beliefs—undermines her loyalty. Her internal narrative, which casts the cursed Mask as a divine sign, is antithetical to Imperial piety, making her a significant security liability.
