
**SUBJECT PROFILE**

Recipient: [[Lord Inquisitor Seraphina Valeria]]
**DESIGNATION**: Judicar Helbrecht Nullis
**CLASSIFICATION**: Sanctioned Ecclesiarchal Enforcer – Exiled Status
**STATUS**: In solitary confinement beneath Bastion-Crypt Durex Sanctum; sermonizing under Null-Field restraint

**EVALUATION**
Once among the most effective roving Judicars of the Helion Reach, subject was stripped of rank after exhibiting unauthorized discretionary mercy and doctrinal deviance. Faith remains unbroken. Fire remains active.

**Zealous Record of Purge**
**Helbrecht Nullis executed eight full-scale burns across hive shrines in the Helion Reach, including Hive Perdition and Duras IV. High casualty rates included sanctioned clergy, unregistered mutants, and civilian dependents. Subject consistently refused to distinguish by age, claiming “the Emperor’s judgment does not weep.”**

**Deviation through Discernment**
**Notable restraint displayed during xenos-adjacent incidents. On Vardis Prime, Nullis spared a shrine marked by ancient Eldar glyphs. On Syrax IV, he observed Tau diplomatic envoys without immediate purge, instead executing known sympathizers post-interaction. The Ecclesiarchy ruled such restraint heretical; Nullis insists “discernment is a blade, not a shield.**

**Necron Encounter – Severe Psychological Impact**
Survived direct contact with awakened Necron units during an unsanctioned relic retrieval. Witness testimonies describe Nullis retreating under prayer, wounded and incoherent. Post-event writings equate Necrons to “the absence of soul, the Emperor's inverse, death that thinks.” Subject's recollection displays trauma yet intensified devotion.

**Interrogation Outcome**
Detained following accusations of xenos complicity and unsanctioned mercy. Subject offered neither denial nor justification—only continuous recitation of penitential litanies. Despite prolonged confinement, spiritual decay has not been observed. Faith remains fortified.

**Recommended Ecclesiarchal Actions  
1. **Continued Null-Field Confinement:** Maintain isolation and suppression protocols. Subject’s presence incites doctrinal unrest among adjacent clergy. Weekly recitation logs to be reviewed for heretical phrasing.  
    ‍
2. **Limited Contact Authorization:** Inquisitorial visitation permitted under strict sigil-cleared escort. Subject remains cooperative, but his rhetorical charisma may sway the uncertain.  
    ‍
3. **Doctrinal Reconciliation Consideration:** Though exiled, subject’s operational record is undeniably effective. Reinstatement under probationary Inquisitorial command may yield decisive results in fringe operations where standard purity cannot survive.  
    ‍
4. **Containment Termination (Conditional):** Should subject display ideological escalation, uncontrolled psychic resonance (documented following Necron contact), or attempt escape, full dissolution rites are authorized.  
    ‍

**Final Arbites Declaration  
_"Helbrecht Nullis is both blade and burn mark—carved deep into the parchment of Imperial doctrine. He is no longer useful to the Ecclesiarchy, for he sees too much and spares too selectively. But to the Inquisition… where fire must walk among shadow… he may be precisely what survives."
