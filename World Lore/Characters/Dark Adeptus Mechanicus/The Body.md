## **Inquisitorial Intelligence Briefing – Cult Designate: “The Body”**

**Document Code:** IX-XN/CULT-DS-998.91-B 
**Classification:** Omega-Level Threat – Active Subsurface Cell 
**Filed By:** Interrogator-Magos Virell, Ordo Xenos 
**Verified By:** Lord Inquisitor Seraphina Valeria **Status:** Escalated – Containment Incomplete

### I. **Cult Overview**

**Designation:** _The Body_ 
**Affiliation:** Dark Mechanicus (Fragmented Cell Network) 
**Operational Duration:** Estimated >3,000 standard years 
**Primary Objective:** Detonation of Nova Bomb Array within Luna Tenebris mantle to release _Midnight Wraith_ from lunar entrapment

### II. **Historical Context**

- First recorded encounter occurred **2,000 years ago**, when a Dark Angels Terminator squad engaged subterranean cult elements beneath Crater Varnax.
- Engagement resulted in partial cult dispersal and long-term behavioral shift:
    - **Conflict aversion** protocols adopted
    - **Hyper-paranoia** embedded in operational logic
    - **Cellular isolation** enforced across all nodes
- Despite setbacks, _The Body_ persisted, adapting its rituals and infrastructure to avoid detection.
- Cult members began **surface rituals** within the shadowpine forests, often during planetary eclipses. These rites are believed to be **psychic synchronizations** with the Dark Soul shard embedded in _Citadel Obscura_.

### III. **Biological and Cybernetic Profile**

- Remaining organic tissue within cult leadership has been **fully overtaken** by the Dark Soul’s influence.
- Subjects exhibit:
    - **Non-verbal communion** with corrupted fauna
    - **Glyphic bleeding** from dermal layers
    - **Memory fragmentation** and shared hallucinations
    - **Rift-aligned neural patterns** incompatible with standard cogitator logic
- Cult members refer to themselves as **“limbs”**, with the five leaders known as **“The Heads”**.

### IV. **Nova Bomb Protocol – Activation Mechanism**

- The Body’s master plan involves the **detonation of a series of Nova Bombs** embedded deep within the lunar mantle.
- These devices are designed to fracture the moon’s crust and sever the gravitational tether holding _Midnight Wraith_ in place.
    
- **Security Protocol:**
    - Activation requires a **2000-digit access code**, split across **five engraved metallic skulls** belonging to the cult’s leaders.
    - Each skull contains a unique segment of the code, etched in micro-glyphic script and encrypted via psychic resonance.
        
- **Current Status:**
    - **Two skulls remain active and accounted for**
    - **Three skulls lost** due to:
        - Apostolic beast attacks
        - Internal betrayal
        - Environmental collapse within Sublevel Theta-9

> _Note: Without all five skulls, activation is impossible. However, cult members are believed to be attempting code reconstruction via corrupted data fragments and ritual memory loops._

### V. **Threat Assessment**

|**Threat Vector**|**Status**|**Notes**|
|---|---|---|
|Nova Bomb Array|Dormant|Devices confirmed via seismic scan; activation pending|
|Cult Activity|Active|Surface rituals increasing in frequency|
|Dark Soul Influence|Escalating|Glyphic contamination spreading to native fauna|
|Apostolic Manifestation|Probable|Multiple sightings of non-native entities near ritual sites|
|Skull Recovery Attempts|Ongoing|Cult deploying autonomous diggers and psychic scouts|

### VI. **Containment Recommendations**

- **Do not engage directly.** Cult members possess unpredictable psychic defenses.
- **Intercept surface rituals.** These events offer rare windows into cult hierarchy.
- **Recover lost skulls.** Priority Alpha – prevent code reconstruction.
- **Monitor seismic activity.** Any spike may indicate bomb priming.
- **Deploy null-field drones.** Suppress glyphic resonance in forest zones.

**Final Annotation – Ordo Xenos:**

> “The Body is not a cult. It is a mechanism. Each limb moves with purpose. Each head dreams of release. And the moon itself listens.”
