**Occupied:** [[House V - Karsen Family]]
**Role:** Foremen

The Karsens were among the first arrivals and acted as informal foremen for the mining effort. They were practical, inward-looking, and resistant to change. Records indicate multiple unexplained deaths over a ten-year span: accidents without clear mechanical failure, falls in familiar tunnels, and one individual found deceased in their quarters with no signs of trauma.

Later logs suggest increasing paranoia. Family members reinforced their living space beyond regulation and stopped attending communal gatherings. The final entry attributed to the house is a maintenance note correcting an alignment error that did not exist.
