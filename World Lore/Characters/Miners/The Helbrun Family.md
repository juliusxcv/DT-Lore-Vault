**Occupied:** [[House I - Helbrun Family]]
**Role:** Surveyors

The Helbruns spent the most time outside the habitation zone. Their records describe the moon’s lighting as disorienting but manageable—until exposure durations increased due to staffing shortages.

Psychological evaluations note emotional flattening and delayed responses. One individual began reporting that shadows appeared before their sources. Another stopped speaking altogether.

Two confirmed deaths occurred during routine surveys under conditions that should not have been fatal. The remaining family members left abruptly, taking survey equipment but no personal effects.
