**Occupied:** [[House IV - Valev Family]]
**Role:** Record Keepers

The Valevs were meticulous record-keepers and maintained much of the settlement’s operational documentation. Their decline is one of exhaustion rather than violence. Personal journals describe persistent headaches, disorientation, and sleep cycles collapsing into irregular waking periods.

Several members exhibited fixation on the moon’s diffuse twilight, describing it as “never changing, yet never still.” One individual removed all interior lighting, claiming it was unnecessary.

The family departed together. No notice was filed, and no transport record exists. Their house was left orderly, with tools cleaned and stored.
