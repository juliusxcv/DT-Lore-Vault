**Occupied:** [[House VI - Morholt Family]]
**Role:** Medicae

The Morholts served as informal medicae, despite lacking full Adeptus training. Their journals provide the clearest account of settlement decline: insomnia, hallucinations, mood instability, and increasing apathy.

They attempted treatment regimens—light therapy, enforced sleep cycles, chemical aids—but documented diminishing returns. The final entries express uncertainty over whether symptoms were environmental or psychological.

The family departed last. One member stayed behind voluntarily, citing duty to “close things properly.” That individual was never found.
