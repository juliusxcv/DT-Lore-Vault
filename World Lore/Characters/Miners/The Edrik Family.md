**Occupied:** [[House III - Edrik Family]]
**Role:** Power Distribution

The Edriks were responsible for keeping the settlement livable: power couplings, air recyclers, heat exchangers, and light regulators. They lived closest to the infrastructure spine, and their house shows the most evidence of continual modification and patchwork repair.

Early records describe them as calm and pragmatic. Later entries, however, reveal an increasing obsession with balance—light levels, airflow ratios, temperature differentials. Adjustments became more frequent and increasingly minor, as if perfection were always just one correction away.

One family member began logging the same maintenance cycle multiple times a day, each entry slightly different, each claiming the previous was “incorrect.” Arguments arose over whose settings were correct. Eventually, no one would touch the controls without rewriting the entire procedure first.

The Edriks departed in fragments. One left with the Valevs. Another vanished between shifts. The last two remained long enough to power down nonessential systems across the settlement—methodically, correctly—before abandoning their house with the breakers left accessible, as if expecting someone else to finish the job.
