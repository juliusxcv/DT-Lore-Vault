
## LVL 1 Autopsy:
Beneath the mutation, the skeletal frame matches that of Luna Tenebris’ native predators—canid in structure, though heavily distorted. The spine has erupted outward into jagged growths, while bone clusters bloom across the torso like a spreading infection, suggesting uncontrolled regenerative processes rather than deliberate design. Whatever altered this creature did not replace the native species—it forced it to outgrow itself, until even its own body became a weapon.
