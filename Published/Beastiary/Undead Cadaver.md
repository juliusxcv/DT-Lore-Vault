## Autopsy Reports:

## LVL 1 Autopsy:
Decay is present, but not progressive. The tissues are locked in a state between rot and function, sustained by an unknown signal. When isolated, the body slackens as if waiting for permission to exist. Could these be former Tenebris Research Personnel?

## LVL 2 Autopsy:
Cellular analysis reveals a state of arrested necrosis: degradation and structural preservation occur simultaneously. Crystalline micro-filaments—foreign to human biology—are embedded throughout the tissue, acting as conduits for the animating signal rather than repairing damage. Behavioral observation confirms complete loss of individual cognition; however, units display increased aggression and coordination in proximity, suggesting a shared signal network. Without higher-order control entities, they revert to aimless, stimulus-driven movement, reinforcing their classification as biologically animated drones

## LVL 3 Autopsy:
Residual engram traces indicate these were indeed once Tenebris personnel. With fragmented neural echoes of fear and memory still present but non-functional. The crystalline micro-filaments  do not reanimate in the conventional sense. They preserve just enough structure to enforce movement and obedience. This is not warp reanimation, but a controlled suspension of death itself.
