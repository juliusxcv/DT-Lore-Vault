**Occupied:** [[House II - Renn Collective]]
**Role:** Logistics and Ration Oversight

The Renn household consisted of multiple branches and was the most socially active. They attempted to maintain morale through scheduled meals, observances, and use of the chapel. As conditions worsened, they became increasingly rigid in routine.

Logs mention arguments over perceived slights and accusations of neglect or sabotage, none substantiated. Two members were restrained for “erratic behavior” and later released without further incident.

Eventually, half the household left with a scheduled ore shipment and never returned. The remaining members stayed for several months before abandoning the house in stages.
