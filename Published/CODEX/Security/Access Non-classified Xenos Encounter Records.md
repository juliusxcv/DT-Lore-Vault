XENOS ENCOUNTER RECORD 11-THETA
Designation: “Silent Watchers”
Classification: NON-CLASSIFIED – FIELD INCIDENT
Origin: Shadow Sentinel Patrol Sigma-4
Location: Theta Perimeter Ridge, Luna Tenebris
Date: 993.M41.174
Status: Archived – No Further Action Required

**Incident Summary
During routine perimeter patrol approximately 4.2 kilometers east of Theta Communications Relay, Patrol Sigma-4 reported visual contact with unidentified humanoid figures positioned along elevated basalt ridges overlooking the relay compound.

Observers described the entities as:
Tall, slender silhouettes
Pale armor or reflective garments
Minimal thermal signature
Movement inconsistent with standard human locomotion
Initial auspex sweeps returned intermittent contact only.

No hostile actions were observed.

**Patrol Testimony
“Thought they were statues at first. Three of them. Standing too still. Then one moved—smooth like smoke. We raised optics and they were already looking at us.”
— Trooper Selk, Patrol Sigma-4

“Didn’t look like raiders. Didn’t look lost either. More like they were checking if we were still here.”
— Sergeant Varo Mesk

**Response
Standard contact protocol enacted.
Vox challenge issued in Low Gothic and High Gothic
No reply received
Long-range illumination deployed
Patrol advanced to investigate
Upon reaching the ridge, no entities remained.
No tracks discovered.
No signs of infiltration or sabotage detected.

**Preliminary Assessment
Visual characteristics tentatively align with known Aeldari physiognomy.
Intent remains undetermined.
Given absence of aggression, command assessment classified the incident as non-hostile observation.
No escalation authorized.

**Administrative Annotation
Repeated sightings of distant unidentified figures have been logged sporadically across Luna Tenebris perimeter sectors. Current standing doctrine advises continued monitoring without engagement unless hostile intent is demonstrated.
