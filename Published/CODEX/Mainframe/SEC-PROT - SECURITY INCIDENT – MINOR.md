**SECURITY INCIDENT – MINOR**  
**Origin:** Garrison Barracks  

**Classification:** Low Priority Disciplinary Record

> Two enlisted personnel observed wagering on the number of “god-machines” housed below fabrication sectors.  
> Discipline measures issued for rumor propagation and disrespect toward Mechanicus property.

---

### Incident Summary

During second-shift recreation period within Barracks Hall C, two enlisted personnel engaged in unauthorized speculation concerning compartmentalized fabrication projects and restricted freight activity.

Identified personnel:

- Junior Enginseer **Lyr-09**
- Enginseer Candidate **Pell-22**

Conversation reportedly followed repeated observation of oversized freight movements through lower manufactorum sectors.

---

### Witness Statement – Trooper Garrick Hale

> “Wasn’t serious trouble. Just the two cogboys arguing again.  
> Lyr said there was one engine below us. Pell swore there were two.  
> Then they started betting ration chits on reactor size and weapon loadouts.”

---

### Partial Conversation Record

**Lyr-09:**

> “You don’t build freight rails that large for tanks.”

**Pell-22:**

> “And you don’t route enough shielding for a single engine either.”

**Lyr-09:**

> “I’m telling you—the one with the weapon is real. Heard Line IX workers talking about recharge cycles longer than a plasma siege battery.”

**Pell-22:**

> “Then it needs escort. No god-machine carries that much wrath alone.”

**Lyr-09:**

> “Maybe that’s what the second one is for.”

**Pell-22:**

> “Protection?”

**Lyr-09:**

> “Shielding. What else?”

**Unknown Trooper:**

> “Or maybe you two have been breathing welding fumes again.”

---

### Security Findings

No classified data breach detected.

Personnel possessed no verified access to restricted fabrication records and appear to have derived conclusions from observation of freight activity, reactor allocation rumors, and manufactorum gossip.

Rumor propagation nevertheless risks disruption of compartmentalized security practices.

---

### Disciplinary Action

Punitive measures issued:

- Two-cycle maintenance reassignment
- Suspension of recreational privileges
- Formal reprimand for disrespectful reference to Mechanicus assets as _“parking-lot Titans”_

No further action recommended.

---

### Security Command Note

Persistent rumors concerning “god-machines,” oversized freight, and restricted reactor activity continue to circulate among lower-clearance personnel.

Staff are reminded:

> Restricted fabrication programs do not concern garrison personnel.  
> Speculation serves no operational purpose.

**Final Classification**
