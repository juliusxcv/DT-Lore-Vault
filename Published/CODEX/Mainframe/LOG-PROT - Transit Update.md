### Theatre Notes – Bellum Eternum Tertius

Latest convoy recovered material from the **Northern Ash Front**, where Imperial forces continue clearance operations against the remaining warbands of the **Skarrgrim Wartribe**.

Field reports indicate the primary greenskin strongholds have been broken following nine years of attritional warfare.

Residual resistance persists in ash canyons, refinery ruins, and subterranean scrap-fortifications.

Imperial command classifies the conflict as _strategically contained_.

---

### Vehicle Recovery Notes

Recovered combat hulls include:

- 713 Leman Russ variants
- 2403 Chimera transports
- 1275 Destroyed Sentinel frame
- 30 unidentified armored hulls pending Mechanicus classification

Several vehicles displayed extensive improvised Ork modification, including welded ram assemblies, secondary engine grafts, and unstable fuel systems.

Reverse-analysis teams requested immediate access before decontamination procedures.

---

### Delta Gate Observations

Convoy personnel report reduced greenskin coordination compared to prior campaign cycles.

Captured vox fragments suggest fragmentation within surviving Ork command structures following the death of multiple warboss claimants.

One field officer described current conditions as:
> “No proper army left. Just packs of killers fighting us and each other over the bones.”

---

### Recovery Team Remarks

Multiple wounded guardsmen exhibited severe burn trauma, fungal contamination, and crude ballistic injuries consistent with close-range Ork assault tactics.

Three survivors carried battlefield trophies believed taken from enemy Nob leadership.

Medicae personnel reminded intake staff that trophies remain subject to contamination protocols.
