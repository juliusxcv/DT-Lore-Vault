### Patrol Summary

Routine tunnel inspection along Freight Junction IV interrupted following activation of priority transit alarms.

Patrol ordered to establish perimeter and suspend civilian and maintenance traffic.

Convoy emerged from Deep Rail Access under escort authority verified by Security Command and Magenta clearance.

Observed security measures included:

- Shadow Sentinel escort detachment
- Armed Skitarii support personnel
- Servo-skull surveillance cordon
- Local noospheric blackout field
- Temporary lumen suppression within adjoining corridors

No transit violations recorded.

---

### Convoy Description

Convoy consisted of multiple heavy transport cradles operating on oversized subterranean rail infrastructure not listed within standard patrol routing archives.

Estimated characteristics:

- Total convoy length exceeding 240 meters
- Triple-reinforced motive rail system
- Multiple plasma-hauler support units
- Severe tunnel clearance restrictions

Transport dimensions required temporary removal of maintenance equipment and evacuation of adjacent service corridors.

Patrol noted unusually low transit speed and continuous reactor shielding presence.

Claim unverified.
