**INTERNAL SECURITY MEMO**  
**Origin:** Security Command Deck  

**Distribution:** All Facility Personnel / Logistics Staff / Maintenance Divisions

> Personnel are advised not to interfere with Shadow Sentinel escort details moving between Beta Gate and Armory Sanctum.  
> Their schedules, cargo, and destinations are compartmentalized by Magos decree.

---

### Escort Protocol Reminder

The **Shadow Sentinel Regiment** remains the designated primary security force assigned to Luna Tenebris and associated transit infrastructure.

Escort movements involving Shadow Sentinel personnel are to be treated as sealed operational activity.

Personnel are reminded:
- Do not obstruct escort routes
- Do not request cargo manifests
- Do not alter transit scheduling without Security Command authorization
- Do not engage escort commanders regarding operational purpose

Escort details operate under standing authority independent of local logistical oversight.

---

### Security Function

Shadow Sentinel deployments are routinely assigned to:
- Classified cargo transfer
- Armory and prototype security
- Beta and Delta Gate convoy protection
- Arboretum livefire supervision
- Internal containment and emergency response

Their movement through facility sectors should not be interpreted as evidence of security breach or active threat condition.

---

### Identification Notice

Personnel unfamiliar with Sentinel operations should note the following identifiers:
- Matte blue combat armor
- Refractor-assisted concealment systems
- Regimental insignia clearance verified by Security Command
- Authorization to carry unrestricted weapons within secured sectors

Shadow Sentinel personnel are not required to surrender armaments while on duty inside Tenebris.

---

### Conduct Advisory

Recent reports indicate nonessential personnel attempting to observe escort activity or speculate regarding cargo destination.

This behavior is discouraged.

Shadow Sentinel operational secrecy exists by Mechanicus and Imperial decree.

Repeated interference may result in disciplinary review or temporary revocation of sector access privileges.

---

### Security Command Remarks

The Shadow Sentinels have maintained uninterrupted defense of Luna Tenebris through hostile surface conditions, xenos incursions, and classified recovery operations.

Their continued presence is considered essential to facility security and strategic stability.

Questions regarding Sentinel deployment are unnecessary.

---

**Final Directive**

> Observe escort cordons.  
> Yield transit priority when instructed.  
> Allow the Sentinels to perform their duty.
