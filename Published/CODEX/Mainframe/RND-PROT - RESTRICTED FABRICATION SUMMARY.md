### Fabrication Overview

While officially designated a biological research installation, Tenebris retains extensive secondary manufactorum capability inherited from earlier logistical and military support operations.

Primary fabrication activity includes:

- Prototype armored platforms
- Experimental weapons systems
- Battlefield recovery and reverse-analysis
- Limited production of specialist military equipment
- Restricted Mechanicus development contracts

Manufactorum output remains intentionally low-volume and high-complexity.

Tenebris is not classified as a mass-production forge.

---

### Strategic Function

Bellum Eternum Tertius conflict zones continue to provide steady acquisition opportunities.

Recovered materiel includes:
- Disabled Imperial armor
- Captured enemy machinery
- Weapons requiring redesign or purification
- Battlefield salvage unsuitable for conventional forge reclamation

Recovered systems are routinely dismantled, analyzed, and repurposed for approved development initiatives.

Waste losses remain below sector average.
