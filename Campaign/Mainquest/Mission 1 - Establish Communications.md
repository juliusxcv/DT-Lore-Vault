### Setup
The players arrive with a Valkyrie at the corner of the map. The Landing Pad is occupied by the Corvus Blackstar from the Killteam, so the players chose where to land.

In the middle of the map is the research base. Badly damaged but not derelict, some signs of working system are still there.

The base is surrounded by long abandoned mining huts, it appears the Research Base was built in the middle of a dig site.

Near the watchtower at the canyon there is the Vox Relay Tower.

**Objectives:**
- Vox Relay Tower is repaired with a welding kit.
- 
- Access Code for auxiliary power conduits is entered in command room. 
	- This powers up the remaining Generator.
- Access Code for "reboot protocol for internal vox relays" is entered in command room.
	- This triggers the first wave of Undead.
- Vox Room is operated by a PC and the PC initiates the [[VOX-4ZT - Orbital Relay Ping Protocol]]
