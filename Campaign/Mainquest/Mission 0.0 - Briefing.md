### Mission Briefing

> _Listen closely, for I will not repeat myself. I am Lord Inquisitor Seraphina Valeria of the Ordo Xenos, and I have summoned you here for a mission that will determine your fate._
> 
> _You stand accused of harboring ties to xenos filth, betraying the trust of the Imperium. Whether these accusations hold merit is irrelevant now; your lives hang by the thinnest of threads. There is no escape from this task, and your survival depends on your success._
> 
> _Your mission will be to gather Information on what has happened to the Killteam Velatum Umbra and what has transpired in that facility. Your primary task upon arriving will be to restore power to the facility and repair the Vox Relay, ensuring that communication with the Imperium can be reestablished. Your secondary task will be to get the topside installation up and running again._
> 
> _Only then will i be able to assist you from orbit._
> 
> _This is no easy task; the installation has lain dormant for months, and the dangers within its walls could be numerous and relentless._
> 
> _Should you meet any personnel still alive on the site, expect them to be in very rough shape. Our cogitators project that they must have run out of resources quite some time ago. From the little we could decipher most of the personnel has been underground when the lockdown protocol came into effect._
> 
> _The base was once protected by an elite Regiment of Imperial Guard Soldiers named the Shadow Sentinels. One of the last transmissions from the Killteam reported that some were still alive._
> 
> _Understand this: When Tenebris went into Lockdown the System Lords of Bellum Eternum have been too proud to inform us in time. Thus preventing us from adressing this situation properly. As it stands I do not expect you to make it far into the base initially. I will assist you with how i can with supplies and even personnel... should you prove your worth._
> 
> _There is one catch. Time is working against you. Despite insisting that this matter falls under the jurisdiction of the Inquisition we are expecting a heavy combat patrol to arrive soon._
> 
> _The Chapter in question is none other than the Black Templars. And once they are here, rest assured, will show no mercy to those who fail in their duty. Their reputation for relentless pursuit and uncompromising justice is well known. They will not hesitate to cleanse the base—and you—if they find any hint of xenos corruption or failure._
> 
> _Your success in this mission is our only chance to find out what has happened at Luna Tenebris and your only chance at redemption. Prove your worth to the Imperium, and perhaps you will earn a sliver of mercy. Fail, and you will not live to see the light of another day._
> 
> _Now, prepare yourselves and begin your descent to Luna Tenebris._
> 
> _May the Emperor guide your actions._
