## Setup
The players arrive with a Valkyrie at the corner of the map. The Landing Pad is occupied by the Corvus Blackstar from the [[Killteam Velatum Umbra]], so the players chose where to land.

The Research post is staffed with [[Shadow Sentinels]] who immediately start to fire on the PC's. The players either kill them all OR they manage to convince them that they are on the same side. If convinced, the Guards will act as sentries until dead.

Convincing the Guards:
- Psyker can infiltrate the mind of the Leader. Make a Roll    
- If within 5" any member of the Party can show the Guards the Sigil of the Inquisition. The Guards will then immediately cease their hostility.

_Their task: to secure and reestablish the [[Research Facility Tenebris]] and to uncover the fate of the [[Killteam Velatum Umbra]] gone silent._

_Located deep within the dense, shadowy forests, the research base has lain dormant for decades, its halls echoing with the whispers of the past._

_As the Valkyrie thunders its way towards the eerie, fog-shrouded landscape, the team sits in silence. Each rumble hammering in the reality of the situation._

_There is no escape. And the cold and unforgiving claws of the inquisition would not relent until they have fulfilled their purpose._

_To restore power, the Team must bypass ancient security systems, navigate the base's labyrinthine corridors, and repair the damaged generators._

_Fixing the Vox Relay requires them to ascend the vox tower, braving the hazardous climb and reactivating the machinery._

_As dawn breaks over the moon's horizon, casting a pale light through the forest canopy, the group finally catches a glimpse of their objective. It is going to be a long day.


In the middle of the map is the research base. Badly damaged but not derelict, some signs of working system are still there.

The base is surrounded by long abandoned mining huts, it appears the Research Base was built in the middle of a dig site.

Near the watchtower at the canyon there is the Vox Relay Tower.


**Players win when:**
- Vox Relay Tower is repaired and Powered
- Vox Relay Tower: 18/18 HP
- Generator 1: 10/10 HP
- Generator 2: 10/10 HP
