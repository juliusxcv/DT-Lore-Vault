- This Mission introduces the Zombie Spawns which will ramp up
- This Mission introduces Supply Drops.

The players win when:
- The Boss is defeated

**Escalation**

- **Wave 1:** Start with weaker foes to give the party a warm-up and establish the stakes. Think small squads or scattered enemies. 
  Type: [[I - Undead Legionnaires]]  

- **Wave 2–3:** Increase either the enemy count, their quality (e.g., elites or specialized units), or introduce new enemy types with tricky abilities. 
  Type: [[I - Undead Legionnaires]], [[II - Moonspawned Aberrant]], [[II - Necromutant]]

- **The Sentinels arrive**
  The Shadow Sentinels arrive in a cohesive unit from the Landing Platform Corner. If there are Undead Legionnaires in their Sightlines they will prioritize those. Otherwise they will start open fire on the players until convinced otherwise.
  
- Concincing the Sentinels
- Psyker can infiltrate the mind of the Leader. Make a Roll
- If close enough any member of the Party can show the Guards the Sigil of the Inquisition. The Guards will then immediately cease their hostility.

#### Supplydrops

> _The vox speakers crackle to life, cutting through the chaotic battlefield as Inquisitor Seraphina Valeria’s voice carries across the base—clear, sharp, and unwavering despite the situation._
> 
> **Seraphina:** _"Reinforcements are unavailable. You will hold your ground. However, I am authorizing supply drops—munitions, medical kits, and heavy weapons where possible."_
> 
> (_She pauses for a brief moment before continuing, her tone shifting to a stern warning._)
> 
> _"Be advised: accuracy is compromised. Atmospheric interference and mechanical disruptions may result in drops landing outside the base perimeter._ If your forces require resupply, you will _retrieve it yourself._"
> 
> (_Static flickers through the transmission, but her voice remains controlled._)
> 
> _"Adapt, overcome, and maintain position. I expect results. Valeria out."_
 

- **Supplydrop**: Depending on how the Party is doing the Supplydrop will have Equipment (Stims, Explosives).
  
- **Wave 4–5:** Add environmental effects (e.g., limited visibility, hazards, or terrain changes) to keep the players thinking on their feet. Introduce mini-bosses, perhaps enemy leaders or beasts. 
  Type: [[I - Undead Legionnaires]], [[II - Moonspawned Aberrant]], [[II - Shard Swarm]]

- **Wave 6 (Final Wave):** Build to overwhelming odds.
  Type: [[I - Undead Legionnaires]], [[II - Moonspawned Aberrant]], [[II - Shard Swarm]], [[III - Ravager Husk]]
   
- **Wave 7 (Boss):**  
   Type: [[III - Grave Titan]]


**Resource Management**
Restrict the party’s access to rest or supplies, but strategically scatter limited healing or ammunition resupplies after key waves. This forces players to strategize and ration their resources. Zombies and Mutated Wildlife have Target Priority on the Generators and will attack them as soon as they come within 8" Range. Without Generators the Systems of the Base will Turn Off.
