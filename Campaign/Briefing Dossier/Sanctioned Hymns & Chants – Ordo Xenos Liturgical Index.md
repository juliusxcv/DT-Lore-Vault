### _Litany of the Iron Soul_
**Use:** To resist psychic intrusion or warp influence 

**Recitation:**
> _“My mind is a fortress. My thoughts are iron. My will is the Emperor’s flame.”_ 
> _“No whisper shall enter. No shadow shall linger. I am His silence.”_

### _Hymn of the Hollow Light_
**Use:** To restore morale in low-light or isolation conditions 

**Recitation:**
> _“Though the stars are veiled, His gaze remains.”_ 
> _“Though the path is lost, His hand guides mine.”_ 
> _“Though I walk in shadow, I carry His flame.”_

### _Canticle of the Bound Flame_
**Use:** To invoke inner strength during physical or emotional collapse 

**Recitation:**
> _“The fire within me is not mine—it is His.”_ 
> _“Let it burn. Let it consume. Let it rise.”_ 
> _“I am the vessel. I am the pyre. I am the light.”_

### _Chant of the Sealed Gate_
**Use:** To ward off xenos presence or seal a location 

**Recitation:**
> _“By His will, I close the gate.”_ 
> _“By His blood, I seal the breach.”_ 
> _“By His name, I deny the unclean.”_

### _Litany of the Last Breath_
**Use:** For moments of imminent death or sacrifice 

**Recitation:**
> _“This breath is not mine—it is His.”_ 
> _“This blood is not mine—it is His.”_ 
> _“This death is not mine—it is His victory.”_

### _Hymn of the Forgotten Watch_
**Use:** When abandoned, lost, or cut off from command 

**Recitation:**
> _“They may forget me. He never will.”_ 
> _“They may leave me. He never shall.”_ 
> _“I am not alone. I am His sentinel.”_

### _Canticle of the Void-Eclipsed_
**Use:** To resist despair in the presence of xenos artifacts or psychic anomalies 

**Recitation:**
> _“The void speaks lies. I speak truth.”_ 
> _“The stars blink. I do not.”_ 
> _“I am the echo of His voice. I am the silence that follows.”_
