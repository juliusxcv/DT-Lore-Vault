---

I. Overview

Luna Tenebris is the second natural satellite of Bellum Eternum Tertius, located within the Bellum Eternum system. The moon possesses a stable temperate climate, low atmospheric volatility, and a unique orbital alignment resulting in perpetual twilight conditions. Despite initial classification as a potential resource site, Luna Tenebris was ultimately deemed of miniscule strategic value due to limited mineral yield, logistical inefficiencies, and adverse psychological effects on personnel.

---

II. Historical Utilization

A. Initial Survey and Colonization (M38.992–M39.014)
- Conducted under the auspices of the Adeptus Mechanicus Explorator Corps.
- Surface scans revealed moderate deposits of ferrous and silicate materials.
- Mining operations established.
- Civilian and penal labor contingents deployed for excavation and infrastructure development.

B. Logistical Role in Bellum Eternum Conflict (M39.015–M40.221)
- During the protracted war between Imperial Guard contingents and Ork infestations on Bellum Eternum Tertius, Luna Tenebris served as a secondary logistics hub.
- Functions included:
  - Temporary storage of non-critical munitions.
  - Staging of low-priority supply convoys.
  - Short-term housing for wounded personnel deemed unfit for return to combat.
- No direct combat engagements occurred on Luna Tenebris.
- Strategic value remained negligible; moon was bypassed in all major fleet movements.

C. Decommissioning and Abandonment (M40.222–M40.229)
- Mining operations ceased following resource depletion and repeated structural failures in subterranean shafts.
- Psychological degradation among personnel attributed to ambient light conditions and environmental isolation.
- All remaining Imperial assets withdrawn to Bellum Eternum Prime.
- Research Station Tenebris placed under dormant status; no further development authorized.

---

III. Current Status

- Imperial Classification: Dormant Logistics Node – No Active Deployment  
- Population: Uninhabited (officially)  
- Infrastructure: Derelict; partial structural integrity in select facilities  
- Environmental Hazards: Geological instability, overgrowth, anomalous light distortion  
- Inquisitorial Interest: Elevated due to recent detection of xenos artifact signatures
