EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS – OPERATION

I. Mission Failure Protocol
Definition: Mission failure is defined as the inability or failure to locate and confirm status of missing Astartes, or loss of operational integrity due to death, desertion, or insubordination.

Response Procedure:
1. Immediate Isolation: All surviving operatives are to cease communication with external assets and activate personal locator beacons.
2. Containment Directive: Operatives must destroy all mission-related data, equipment, and samples to prevent compromise.
3. Final Transmission: A brief vox report must be submitted detailing failure parameters. Emotional content is prohibited.
4. Await Termination: Upon confirmation of failure, orbital assets will initiate Protocol Vultus Mortis:
   - Orbital Strike Authorization: Targeted bombardment of last known operative location.
   - Biometric Detonation: Subdermal compliance implants will be remotely activated.
   - Asset Nullification: All records of operative identity will be purged from Imperial archives.

Note: Redemption is not granted through survival. It is earned through success. Failure is not tolerated.

---

II. Contamination Protocol
Definition: Exposure to xenos biological agents, psychic residue, warp-tainted artifacts, or anomalous environmental conditions.

Response Procedure:
1. Quarantine Activation: All exposed operatives must isolate within 30 minutes of suspected contamination.
2. Self-Report: Submit Contamination Form IX-C/44.7 via encrypted vox channel.
3. Purification Measures: Administer standard anti-xenos purgatives (if available). Recite Litany of Cleansing.
4. Containment Beacon: Activate personal beacon with contamination flag. Await retrieval or termination.

Secondary Measures:
- If symptoms include hallucinations, mutation, or loss of cognitive function, operatives are authorized to self-terminate.
- If contamination spreads to equipment or environment, initiate Scorched Earth Protocol using issued incendiary devices.

---

III. Psychic Breach Protocol
Definition: Manifestation of uncontrolled psychic phenomena, contact with warp entities, or possession by non-corporeal intelligences.

Response Procedure:
1. Immediate Silence: Cease all vox transmissions. Psychic breach may propagate through signal.
2. Containment Circle: If trained, operatives may attempt to inscribe a containment sigil using ash, blood, or sanctified ink.
3. Sanctioned Execution: If breach is confirmed, the affected operative must be terminated by squad consensus.
4. Report and Purge: Submit Psychic Breach Form IX-P/77.3. Destroy all personal effects of the afflicted.
