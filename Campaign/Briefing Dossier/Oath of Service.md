### INSTRUMENT OF TEMPORARY SANCTION

_(Common designation: Oath of Usefulness)_

By writ of the Holy Inquisition and under authority vested in the Throne by the God-Emperor of Mankind, the undersigned parties (hereafter **“the Operatives”**) are hereby granted **conditional continuation of existence** for the sole purpose of service to the Imperium of Man.

Acceptance of this Instrument is **mandatory**. Refusal is interpreted as admission of guilt.

---

### I. STATUS OF THE OPERATIVES

1. The Operatives are **not citizens**, **not soldiers**, and **not entitled persons**.
2. The Operatives are designated **Expendable Assets (Provisional)**.
3. Legal personhood is suspended for the duration of service and may be revoked retroactively.

---

### II. PROPERTY AND MATERIAL CLAIMS

1. **All equipment**, weapons, armor, tools, ammunition, stims, rations, relics, scrap, components, biological matter, and salvage **found, issued, fabricated, repaired, modified, carried, touched, or merely observed** during the course of service are the **sole and eternal property of the Imperium of Man**.
    
2. **Expenditure does not imply ownership transfer.**
    - Ammunition fired remains Imperial property.
    - Stimulants injected remain Imperial property.
    - Armor damaged by enemy action remains Imperial property.
3. Any loss, destruction, misuse, or failure to return said property will be recorded as **theft, sabotage, or dereliction**, regardless of circumstances.
    
4. **All outstanding material debts** will be reclaimed:
    
    - Through labor reassignment
    - Through penal servitude
    - Through bodily reclamation
    - Through generational tithe, where applicable

---

### III. BIOLOGICAL AND COGNITIVE CLAIMS

1. The bodies of the Operatives are considered **temporary vessels**.
    
2. Upon death, incapacitation, or loss of usefulness, all remains are subject to:
    - Autopsy
    - Reanimation
    - Servitor conversion
    - Disposal at Inquisitorial discretion
3. Memories formed during service are **classified information**.
    - Unauthorized recollection constitutes data-theft.

---

### IV. SECRECY AND NON-DISCLOSURE

1. The Operatives will not speak of this mission, its objectives, its existence, or its consequences.
2. Silence is required **before**, **during**, and **after** service.
3. Survival does not constitute release from this clause.
4. Breach of secrecy will result in:
    - Immediate termination
    - Post-mortem interrogation
    - Exemplary punishment of associates
---

### V. FAILURE CONDITIONS

Failure includes, but is not limited to:
- Insubordination
- Hesitation
- Unauthorized retreat
- Loss of equipment
- Damage to Imperial assets
- Survival beyond usefulness
- Curiosity exceeding clearance
- Mercy without sanction

Failure will be met with **corrective action**.

---

### VI. DURATION OF SERVICE

1. This Instrument remains in effect **until formally revoked**.
2. Revocation is **not guaranteed**.
3. Completion of objectives does not imply release, pardon, or survival.

---

### VII. FINAL DECLARATION

The Operatives acknowledge that:

- This Instrument is **not a contract**, but a record.
- Guilt is presumed.
- Redemption is improbable.
- Compliance is mandatory.

The Emperor requires tools.  
Tools are used until they break.
